THE CALM CTO

Rebuilding the junior ladder: a framework

1
Hire for different fundamentals
Curiosity, questions, pattern recognition
2
Onboard differently
Understand first, AI later, explain everything
3
Train AI as a teammate
Junior pair programmer, not code writer
4
Redefine "junior work"
Tests, docs, refactoring, internal tools
5
Create AI-specific learning paths
Prompting, context, verification, debugging
6
Measure learning, not shipping
Understanding over PR velocity
7
Maintain mentorship time
More time, not less. Non-negotiable.
8
Plan for tools to change
Evaluate and adapt, not tool-specific
Judgment over speed. Understanding over shipping. Verification over generation.
