Two positions on junior hiring

Position 1
Keep hiring juniors like before
  • + Talent pipeline for future seniors
  • + Cheaper than senior hires
  • + Adopt AI tools faster
  • + Fresh perspectives
Position 2
AI has reduced the need
  • + AI tools provide 30% productivity gains
  • + Junior work is increasingly automated
  • + AI costs £8/month vs £70K salary
  • + No training overhead
Both positions are treating AI as static. The real question: what are we training juniors to do?
